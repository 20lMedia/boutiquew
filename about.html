Our Story

The journey of Elegance Boutique

The Beginning

Founded in 2010 by Priya Sharma, Elegance Boutique began as a small studio in the heart of Bangalore with a vision to create garments that celebrate India's rich textile heritage while embracing contemporary design sensibilities.

What started as a passion project has now grown into one of India's most respected boutique fashion houses, known for its exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our Mission

At Elegance, our mission is to preserve and promote India's rich textile traditions while creating contemporary designs that resonate with the modern woman. We believe in sustainable fashion practices and supporting local artisans and craftspeople.

Each piece in our collection tells a story - of heritage, craftsmanship, and the hands that created it. We strive to create garments that are not just beautiful but also meaningful.

Our Craftsmanship

The art behind our creations

Hand Embroidery

Our skilled artisans use traditional techniques like zardozi, aari, and gota patti work.

Handloom Weaving

We work with master weavers who create exquisite fabrics on traditional looms.

Block Printing

Our block prints are created by hand using carved wooden blocks and natural dyes.

Natural Dyeing

We use traditional plant-based dyes that are eco-friendly and create unique colors.
